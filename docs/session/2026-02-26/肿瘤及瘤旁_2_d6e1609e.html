### **Inflammatory/Mono-like-TAMs represent a transitional state in intratumoral macrophage differentiation**

To delineate the differentiation dynamics of tumor-associated macrophages (TAMs), we performed trajectory inference analysis on 91,223 macrophages spanning eight transcriptionally distinct subpopulations from tumor and peripheral tissues. Diffusion maps were computed on the Harmony-corrected PCA embedding to capture continuous transcriptional gradients.

**PAGA reveals preferential connectivity between Mono-like-TAMs and immunosuppressive lineages.** Partition-based graph abstraction (PAGA) was applied to infer topological relationships among macrophage subpopulations. Inflammatory/Mono-like-TAMs exhibited the strongest connectivity with M2-like-TAMs (connectivity score = 0.476) and Lipid/TREM2-TAMs (0.330), while showing minimal connection to SPP1-TAMs (0.001) (Fig. Xa). This topology suggests that Mono-like-TAMs predominantly give rise to anti-inflammatory macrophage lineages within the tumor microenvironment, rather than the SPP1⁺ subset.

**Diffusion pseudotime positions Mono-like-TAMs at early differentiation stages.** Using diffusion pseudotime (DPT) with the S100A9-highest-expressing Inflammatory/Mono-like-TAM cell as root, we established a continuous differentiation ordering. Inflammatory/Mono-like-TAMs occupied the second earliest pseudotime position (median DPT = 0.099), preceded only by Ribo-TAMs (0.052), while M2-like-TAMs (0.155) and SPP1-TAMs (0.168) were positioned at later stages (Fig. Xb). Notably, tumor-derived SPP1-TAMs exhibited significantly higher pseudotime values than their peripheral counterparts, consistent with tumor-driven terminal differentiation.

**Palantir analysis confirms multi-lineage differentiation potential of Mono-like-TAMs.** Multi-branch pseudotime analysis using Palantir identified four terminal macrophage fates: Lipid/TREM2-TAM, SPP1-TAM, M2-like-TAM, and Homeostatic-TAM. Inflammatory/Mono-like-TAMs displayed the second highest differentiation entropy (mean = 1.137), indicating substantial multipotency (Fig. Xc). Within the Mono-like-TAM compartment, tumor-derived cells showed significantly higher differentiation probability toward M2-like-TAM (Mann-Whitney *P* = 1.82 × 10⁻⁶) and SPP1-TAM (*P* = 8.87 × 10⁻⁹) lineages compared to peripheral-derived counterparts (Fig. Xd), suggesting that the tumor microenvironment actively skews monocyte-to-macrophage differentiation toward immunosuppressive phenotypes.

**Pseudotime-resolved transcriptional dynamics reveal coordinated regulatory programs.** Along the differentiation trajectory, monocyte-associated genes (*S100A9*, *S100A8*, *VCAN*, *FCN1*) were progressively downregulated, while terminal-state markers (*CD163*, *MRC1* for M2-like; *SPP1*, *APOE* for SPP1-TAM) exhibited reciprocal upregulation (Fig. Xe). Transcription factor analysis revealed that early immediate-response factors (*FOS*, *JUN*, *EGR1*, *KLF6*) were replaced by polarization-associated regulators, with *NF1*, *NFKB1*, and *MITF* showing marked upregulation at later pseudotime stages, implicating NF-κB and MITF signaling in driving terminal macrophage polarization (Fig. Xf).

**Tumor microenvironment promotes macrophage differentiation along immunosuppressive trajectories.** Global comparison of pseudotime distributions revealed that tumor-derived macrophages exhibited a significantly broader distribution extending toward later differentiation states compared to peripheral macrophages (Mann-Whitney *P* = 2.56 × 10⁻²², tumor median = 0.336, peripheral median = 0.345) (Fig. Xg). This broader spread, combined with the enrichment of Inflammatory/Mono-like-TAMs in tumor tissue (3,673 tumor vs. 329 peripheral cells), supports a model wherein newly recruited monocytes preferentially differentiate into M2-like and Lipid/TREM2 TAMs under the influence of tumor-derived signals.

RNA velocity 因需要 loom 文件（来自 BAM）而未纳入。是什么文件

Copilot 2026/02/26 23:38

**Loom 文件**（.loom）是一种基于 HDF5 的矩阵存储格式，专门用于存储单细胞基因表达数据。在 RNA velocity 分析中，它需要包含两类特殊的计数矩阵：

| 层 | 含义 | 来源 |
|---|---|---|
| **spliced** | 已剪接的成熟 mRNA 计数 | reads 比对到外显子区域 |
| **unspliced** | 未剪接的前体 mRNA 计数 | reads 比对到内含子区域 |

### 生成流程

原始测序数据 (FASTQ)
    ↓ cellranger / STAR
BAM 文件 (比对结果，含每条 read 的基因组位置)
    ↓ velocyto run (需要 BAM + GTF + repeatmask)
Loom 文件 (含 spliced / unspliced 矩阵)
    ↓ scVelo / velocyto
RNA velocity 分析

### 为什么你的数据无法做

GEO 上公开的 10X 数据通常只提供 **counts 矩阵**（matrix.mtx + barcodes.tsv + genes.tsv），这是已经处理好的 spliced 计数。要区分 spliced/unspliced，必须回到原始的 **BAM 文件**，用 velocyto run 重新统计内含子区域的 reads。

BAM 文件体积非常大（通常每个样本 10-50 GB），GEO 上极少直接提供，需要从 SRA 下载原始 FASTQ 后重新比对，整个流程耗时较长（参考教程中提到生成 loom 约需 8.5 小时）。

**总结**：不是技术上不能做，而是缺少上游的 BAM 文件作为输入。如果需要做 RNA velocity，需要从 SRA 下载原始数据 → FASTQ → cellranger 比对 → BAM → velocyto → loom。

这是 **MAGIC 降噪** 代码，逐步解释：

---

**palantir.utils.run_magic_imputation(adata_mac, dm_res, n_steps=3)**

MAGIC 的核心操作。原理：单细胞数据存在大量 **dropout**（测到 0 但实际有表达），MAGIC 利用扩散图（dm_res）构建细胞间的相似性矩阵，把相邻细胞的表达值加权平均，填补缺失值。n_steps=3 是扩散步数，步数越多平滑程度越高。

---

**可视化部分**

对 4 个关键 marker 基因（S100A9、SPP1、CD163、TREM2），画上下两行对比：

上行 (Raw)：原始稀疏表达，很多细胞点是白色（0值）
下行 (MAGIC)：平滑后的表达，梯度分布更连续清晰

---

**为什么要做这一步？**

原始 scRNA-seq 数据非常稀疏，同一亚群内不同细胞对同一基因的测量值差异巨大，导致：
- UMAP 上 marker 基因的表达点散乱，看不出规律
- 基因趋势曲线（gene trends）噪声很大

MAGIC 降噪后，下游绘制的基因动态曲线会更平滑，生物学规律更清晰。

---

> 注意：MAGIC 降噪的数据**只用于可视化**，不应替换原始数据用于 DEG 分析（会引入偏差）。imp_df 是独立存储的，代码最后也清理了临时写入 obs 的列。
